Free Online Hindi Letter Pad & Productivity Suite

Welcome to National Typing Hub's Official Hindi Letter Pad. Drafting formal letters in Hindi can often be a frustrating experience on standard word processors. Our tool is specifically designed to eliminate the hassle of downloading heavy software. Whether you are preparing for a government clerical exam (like UPSSSC, Rajasthan LDC, or CPCT), or you simply need to submit an official document, our suite gives you everything you need directly in your browser.

Draft Bank Applications & Official Forms Instantly

Time is valuable. That is why we provide meticulously formatted, ready-to-use templates for the most commonly searched documents in India. With a single click, you can load a perfectly structured Bank Application in Hindi (for account transfers or checkbooks), a formal Leave Application format for schools and offices, or a legally compliant RTI Application Format in Hindi (सूचना का अधिकार). Simply replace the bracketed text with your personal details, and you are ready to go.

Advanced Hindi Typing Features: Tables, Hinglish & Inscript

We understand that everyone types differently. Our platform offers distinct ways to draft your document:

  • MS Word Style Tables: Use our smart table generator to instantly insert grids up to 10x10. Easily add or delete rows and columns dynamically while typing inside them.
  • Hinglish Typing (Phonetic): Type naturally in English (e.g., "aapka"), and our smart engine will automatically convert it to Hindi ("आपका") the moment you press the spacebar. Perfect for mobile users and beginners!
  • Inscript Keyboard Scanner: For professional data entry operators, turn on the Inscript toggle to use the official Indian Government typing layout flawlessly. Select from standard Unicode fonts like Mangal, Kokila, or Nirmala UI.
  • Voice Typing (Mic): Tired of typing? Click the microphone and dictate your entire letter aloud. Our speech-to-text engine will transcribe your voice into pristine Mangal Unicode text.

Add Digital Signatures & Export to PDF/Word

A letter isn't official until it's signed. Skip the printer and the scanner—use our Digital Signature Pad to draw your signature directly on your phone screen or upload a pre-scanned stamp/logo. You can even choose between classic Black or official Ballpoint Blue ink. Once your formatting is perfect, download your document securely as a high-resolution PDF or an editable Microsoft Word (DOC) file. Your privacy is guaranteed; all document processing happens locally on your own device.
