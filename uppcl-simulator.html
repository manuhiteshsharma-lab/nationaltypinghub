Frequently Asked Questions (UPPCL Typing)

1. Which font is used in the UPPCL Hindi Typing Test? ▼

As explicitly outlined in the official guidelines, UPPCL uses the legacy, non-Unicode Krutidev 10 / Krutidev 16 font executed via the standard Remington layout[cite: 1]. Unlike other modern exams, Mangal Inscript is NOT permitted.

2. How is Hindi typing speed calculated in UPPCL? ▼

Hindi typing is calculated using the Formula 2 (5-Keystroke Rule)[cite: 1]. Gross Words per Minute is calculated by dividing total keystrokes by 5. Net Words per Minute deducts penalty words (Wrong Characters / 5) from your Gross score.

3. How is English Typing Evaluated? ▼

English typing uses Formula 1. Gross Words are calculated by identifying actual words split by spaces[cite: 1]. Net Words per Minute (NWPM) counts only the fully correct words typed.

4. Is the backspace key permitted during the test? ▼

Yes. UPPCL utilizes "Unrestricted Typing"[cite: 1]. Candidates are allowed to use the backspace and delete keys to correct errors while the timer is running. However, these deletion keystrokes are not counted towards your positive evaluation score.
