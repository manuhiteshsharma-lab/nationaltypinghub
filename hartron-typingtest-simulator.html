Haryana HARTRON SETC Typing Test 2026: Complete Guide

If you are working as a Clerk, Office Associate, Data Entry Operator, or Assistant in any Government Department of Haryana, the State Eligibility Test in Computer Appreciation & Applications (SETC) is a mandatory hurdle you must cross. Conducted by the Haryana State Electronics Development Corporation Limited (HARTRON), qualifying this exam is essential for the grant of increments in your pay scale.

What is the HARTRON SETC Exam Pattern?

The HARTRON SETC exam consists of two primary phases designed to test both your theoretical computer knowledge and your practical execution speed:

  • Part-I (Objective Test): An online test comprising 50 multiple-choice questions in English. The syllabus covers fundamental computer operations, Windows OS, Word Processing, Spreadsheets, PowerPoint, and Internet browsing. You must secure a 50% pass percentage to qualify this section.
  • Part-II (Typing Speed Test): A 5-minute practical typing test conducted directly on a computer. Candidates can choose to appear for the speed test in English, Hindi, or both languages depending on their application preference.

Typing Speed Requirements & Font Details

The typing requirements for the SETC are stringent and require consistent practice. Candidates must achieve a net typing speed of 30 words per minute (WPM) in English or 25 words per minute (WPM) in Hindi.

💡 Crucial Detail for Hindi Typists: Unlike other central exams that may allow Mangal Inscript, the official HARTRON SETC notification strictly mandates that the Hindi typing test shall be conducted in the KRUTI DEV 010 font.

Because Kruti Dev operates on a completely different mechanical mapping system compared to modern Unicode fonts, it is highly recommended that candidates practice on simulators specifically designed to support legacy Kruti Dev typing environments.

How is the SETC Typing Speed Calculated?

HARTRON utilizes a highly specific and transparent mathematical formula to evaluate your final scorecard. Speed is not measured by individual words, but rather by standardizing the length of a word through keystrokes.

  • Standard Word Definition: 1 word = 5 Strokes (key depressions).
  • Gross Speed: (Total Strokes / 5) / Time in Minutes.
  • Mistake Penalty: Total Mistakes / 5.
  • Final Net Speed: Gross Speed - Mistake Penalty.

Exemption from the Online Test (Part-I)

Not everyone needs to take the 50-question objective test. Employees possessing recognized technical qualifications are officially exempted from Part-I. However, the Part-II Typing Test is universally mandatory.

Exam Centers & Final Tips

Tests are generally conducted at HMSDC Gurugram or IDDC Ambala Cantt. Remember to thoroughly verify your e-Admit card, bring your original identity proofs, and ensure your departmental endorsements are complete.

The secret to clearing the SETC HARTRON typing test is establishing muscle memory and keeping your error rate low. A high mistake count severely damages your Net WPM due to the rigid penalty formula. Start practicing daily using a strict 5-minute timer.
