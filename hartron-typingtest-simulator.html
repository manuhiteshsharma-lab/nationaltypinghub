Frequently Asked Questions (UPPCL Typing)

1. Which font is used in the UPPCL Hindi Typing Test? ▼

As explicitly outlined in the official guidelines, UPPCL uses the legacy, non-Unicode Krutidev 10 / Krutidev 16 font executed via the standard Remington layout. Unlike other modern exams, Mangal Inscript is NOT permitted.

2. How is Hindi typing speed calculated in UPPCL? ▼

Hindi typing is strictly calculated using Formula 2 (5-Keystroke Rule). Gross Words per Minute is calculated by dividing total keystrokes by 5. Net Words per Minute deducts penalty words (Wrong Characters / 5) from your Gross score.


GWM =
Total Keystroke Count / 5 Time in Minutes

NWM = Gross Words −
Wrong Characters / 5 Time in Minutes
3. How is English Typing Evaluated? ▼

English typing uses Formula 1. Gross Words are calculated by identifying actual words split by spaces. Net Words per Minute (NWPM) counts only the fully correct words typed.


GWM =
Total number of words typed Time in Minutes

NWM =
Number of correct words typed Time in Minutes
4. Is the backspace key permitted during the test? ▼

Yes. UPPCL utilizes "Unrestricted Typing". Candidates are allowed to use the backspace and delete keys to correct errors while the timer is running. However, these deletion keystrokes are not counted towards your positive evaluation score.
