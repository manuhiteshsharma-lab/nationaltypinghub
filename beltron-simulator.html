Frequently Asked Questions (Beltron DEO)

What are the qualifying marks for Beltron typing? ▼
To pass the Beltron DEO typing test, candidates must achieve a minimum speed of 30 WPM in English or 25 WPM in Hindi. Clearing both gives you an advantage in the merit list.
Which Hindi font and layout does Beltron use? ▼
Beltron exclusively conducts the Hindi typing test using the Mangal Font on the Remington Gail Keyboard Layout. (Kruti Dev is not permitted).
Is the Backspace key permitted during the test? ▼
Yes, full backspace is generally permitted during the Beltron exam, allowing you to correct mistakes. However, excessive corrections will drain your time and lower your final Gross WPM.
How is the Net WPM calculated? ▼
WPM is calculated based on the standard 5-stroke rule (5 keystrokes = 1 word). Net WPM is calculated by taking your total Gross Words and subtracting your penalty errors over the 10-minute duration.
