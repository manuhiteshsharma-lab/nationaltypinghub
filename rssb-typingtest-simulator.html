Rajasthan RSSB Junior Assistant Typing Test: Frequently Asked Questions

How to pass typing test for RSSB Junior Assistant?

To pass the RSSB typing test, you must build strong muscle memory without looking at the keyboard. Practice daily on a simulator that mimics the exact exam environment. Avoid the backspace key to build raw accuracy, and focus heavily on Kruti Dev / Devlys 010 mappings for the Hindi portion.

Is 20 days preparation sufficient to learn typing?

Yes, 20 days is sufficient if you practice with absolute dedication. Spend the first 5 days strictly memorizing the home row and finger placement. Spend the remaining 15 days taking 10-minute mock tests (like the one above) for 2 to 3 hours daily to cross the 30 WPM threshold.

What is the minimum qualifying typing speed for RSSB JA?

In the RSSB exam, speed translates directly into marks. While the minimum qualifying mark is 9 out of 25 per language, aiming for 35+ WPM in English and 30+ WPM in Hindi ensures you secure the maximum possible marks, giving you a massive advantage in the final merit list.
