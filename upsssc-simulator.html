📱 Practice on Mobile without a PC

Preparing for UPSSSC but don't have a laptop? Connect a physical keyboard directly to your smartphone:

  • Purchase a USB Type-C or Micro-USB OTG Adapter.
  • Connect a standard mechanical or membrane keyboard to the adapter, ensuring cables are firmly connected and not loose.
  • Plug the OTG into your phone. The simulator will automatically hide the touch screen keyboard (via inputmode="none") so you can enjoy a full distraction-free typing experience.

Candidate Guidelines

UPSSSC Exam Rules:

  • Minimum Qualifying Speed: 25 WPM for Hindi, 30 WPM for English.
  • Calculations: Net WPM is calculated by subtracting your errors. Accuracy is crucial to pass.
  • Interface: The test will run in full distraction-free mode. No ads or menus will be visible during the timer.
  • Backspace: Allowed, but excessive use will lower your overall Gross Speed.
