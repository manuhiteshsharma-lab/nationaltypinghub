UPSSSC Typing FAQs

Q: Is the UPSSSC typing test qualifying in nature?

Yes, the typing test is purely qualifying. Marks obtained here are not added to your final merit score, but failing either language results in disqualification.

Q: How are Full and Half errors calculated?

A Full Error (1 word penalty) is given for completely misspelled, skipped, or extra words. A Half Error (0.5 word penalty) is given for capitalization, spacing, or punctuation mistakes.

Q: Where can I find official notifications?

Always verify exam dates and admit cards on the Official UPSSSC Website.
