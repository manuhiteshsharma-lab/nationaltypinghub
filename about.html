About National Typing Hub

Our Mission: To provide Indian government job aspirants with a 100% free, highly accurate, and accessible platform to master typing skills and clear their exams with confidence.

Who We Are

National Typing Hub was built out of a necessity. Millions of students across India prepare for prestigious government examinations like SSC CGL, CHSL, UPSSSC, Beltron, and state-level clerk positions. However, finding a reliable, free typing simulator that accurately mimics the strict environment of these exams is incredibly difficult.

We designed this platform to bridge that gap. We focus heavily on the specific requirements of Indian typists, specializing in both English layouts and complex Hindi fonts like Mangal (Inscript) and Kruti Dev 010 (Remington).

What We Offer

  • Professional Speed Testing: A strict-mode typing engine that calculates Gross WPM, Net WPM, and Accuracy exactly how government testing software does.
  • Two-Way Font Conversion: Seamlessly switch text between legacy Kruti Dev formats and modern Unicode (Mangal) text for documentation and practice.
  • Interactive Tutors & Games: Comprehensive visual guides and gamified learning tools designed to build muscle memory for beginners.
  • Mobile Accessibility: Practice on the go. Our platform is optimized to work flawlessly on smartphones when paired with an OTG keyboard.

Why We Are 100% Free

Education and skill development should not be hidden behind paywalls. National Typing Hub is entirely free to use. There are no premium subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no mandatory account registrations. We sustain the platform's server costs through carefully placed, non-intrusive advertisements, ensuring that every student has equal access to professional typing tools.

Get In Touch

We are constantly evolving to meet the needs of our users. If you have suggestions for new features, encounter a bug, or simply want to share your success story after clearing your exam, please reach out to us via our Feedback Page.
