About National Typing Hub

Our Mission: To provide Indian government job aspirants with a 100% free, highly accurate, and accessible platform to master typing skills and clear their exams with confidence.

Who We Are

National Typing Hub was built out of a necessity. Millions of students across India prepare for prestigious government examinations like SSC CGL, CHSL, UPSSSC, Bihar Beltron, and various state-level clerk positions. We understand that finding a reliable, free typing simulator that accurately mimics the strict environment of these exams—especially for regional roles like Rajasthan Informatics Assistant (IA), Sanganak, and Tax Assistant—is incredibly difficult.

We designed this platform to bridge that gap. We focus heavily on the specific requirements of Indian typists, specializing in both English layouts and complex Hindi fonts like Mangal (Unicode) and Kruti Dev 010 / Devlys (Remington). Whether you are training to be a Data Entry Operator, preparing for a Bank Job, or running your own eMitra / Citizen Service Center, we have the tools you need.

What We Offer

  • Professional Speed Testing: A strict-mode typing engine that calculates Gross WPM, Net WPM, and Accuracy exactly how government testing software does.
  • Detailed Error Tracking: After every test, review your specific mistakes to understand your weak points and improve faster. Get a free, shareable certificate upon completion!
  • Two-Way Font Conversion: Seamlessly switch text between legacy Kruti Dev formats and modern Unicode (Mangal) text for documentation and practice.
  • Mobile Accessibility: Practice on the go. Our platform is optimized to work flawlessly on smartphones or tablets when paired with an OTG keyboard or Type-C adapter.

Why We Are 100% Free

Education and skill development should not be hidden behind paywalls. National Typing Hub is entirely free to use. There are no premium subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no mandatory account registrations. We sustain the platform's server costs through carefully placed, non-intrusive advertisements, ensuring that every student has equal access to professional typing tools.
