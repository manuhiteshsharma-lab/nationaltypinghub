Free Online English Letter Pad & Application Maker

Welcome to National Typing Hub's Official English Letter Pad. Drafting professional documents shouldn't require downloading heavy software or paying for subscriptions. Whether you are writing a resignation letter, applying for a bank loan, or drafting a formal complaint, our browser-based word processor provides everything you need in one place.

Ready-to-Use Professional Templates

Staring at a blank page can be intimidating. We have pre-loaded the most common professional formats used in India and globally:

  • Job Application Cover Letters: Perfectly formatted to introduce your resume to HR managers.
  • Resignation Letters: Keep it polite and professional with our standard two-week notice templates.
  • Bank Applications: Quickly request a new ATM card, chequebook, or address change from your branch manager.
  • Leave Applications: Standard formats for schools, colleges, or corporate sick leave.

Add Digital Signatures & Voice Typing

A formal letter isn't complete without a signature. Use our built-in Digital Signature Pad to sign directly on your mobile screen using Black or Blue ink. If you are tired of typing, simply click the Voice Typing (Mic) button and dictate your letter aloud. Our advanced speech-to-text engine will type it out for you perfectly.

Export to PDF or MS Word Instantly

Once your document looks perfect, you can print it directly, download it as a high-resolution PDF for email attachments, or export it as an editable Word Document (DOCX). All processing is done locally on your device, ensuring your private information remains 100% secure.
